Research

  • As an undergraduate, I collaborated with Prof. Bingkai Lin on Parameterized Inapproximability, with Prof. Huacheng Yu on Sketching Complexity, and with Prof. Pinyan Lu on Algorithms with Predictions.

  • During my Ph.D., my research has focused primarily on Hardness of Approximation.
